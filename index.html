About Giorgi Honeli Group

Giorgi Honeli Group is a European supplier of innovative food ingredients and freeze-dried products for the global food industry.

The company combines its European sales and distribution network with the manufacturing capabilities of Ledova LCC, a modern production facility located in Ukraine.

Commissioned in early 2019, Ledova LCC is equipped with advanced processing technologies designed for primary processing of both plant-based products (fruits, berries, and vegetables) and animal-based products (meat and dairy). The facility specializes in vacuum freeze-drying technology, preserving the natural taste, nutritional value, texture, and quality of raw ingredients while significantly extending their shelf life.

Our modern production equipment ensures consistently high product quality and full compliance with international food safety and quality standards.

Through Giorgi Honeli Group, we provide comprehensive sales support, international logistics, and reliable customer service, ensuring efficient cooperation with partners across global markets.

Our Aquafaba Powder — Premium Plant-Based Egg White Substitute.

Our Aquafaba Powder is an innovative and completely natural vegan alternative to egg whites, made from chickpea broth without any preservatives, flavorings, or harmful additives. This clean and functional ingredient is ideal for confectionery, sauces, beverages, and vegan recipes, providing stable foam, light texture, and a natural taste.

The product combines purity, convenience, and functionality: it's easy to store, dose, and delivers consistent performance in any recipe.

Our Aquafaba is the choice for those creating the food of the future: clean, plant-based, and safe.

Production is certified according to FSSC 22000 standards, ensuring quality and compliance with European regulations
